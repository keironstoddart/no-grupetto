What is No Grupetto?

No Grupetto is a cycling community that connects cyclists of similar speeds. Never get dropped on a ride again!

How does it work?

No Grupetto uses machine learning algorithms to analyze your Strava logged rides and match you with other riders of similar skill and close proximity. Make some friends, go on some rides, and come out a better rider than you were before. No Grupetto analyzes your performances in real time to give you better matches as you become a better rider.

Sounds cool to me! How can I join?

Awesome! All you need to join is to give No Grupetto access to your Strava rides. Click the join button below!
